About Me

Hi, I'm Joshua Peres. I'm a 19 year old autistic male. I am currently in my 1st term of my IMD program. SInce the age of 17, I have been working towards getting a job in either mathematics or media arts. I have dabbled in graphic design since my teenage years.
Being autistic, my social life has been significantly different from the average human's. Since a young age, I've struggled with making friends. I've never been someone who enjoys conversations. I have tried to work on it in recent years, however. Due to my beliefs in several fields being so polarizing, I also struggle with keeping friends.
